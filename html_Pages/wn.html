In the Seven Wonders of the Ancient World, ancientmasterpieces such as the Temple of Artemis and the Hanging Gardens of Babylon are remembered. However, the Seven Wonders of the Natural World are proof — if proof is needed — that Mother Nature is just as talented an architect. Compiled by CNN and Seven Natural Wonders in 1997, this list spans all 7 continents, and includes some of the greatest heights of the earth and depths of the oceans, some lesser known sites, and some well-known features that you may have already visited yourself. The seven wonders of the ancient world have been celebrated by scholars, writers, and artists since at least 200 B.C. These marvels of architecture, like Egypt's pyramids, were monuments of human achievement, built by Mediterranean and Middle Eastern empires of their day with little more than crude tools and manual labor.

Today, all but one of these ancient wonders have vanished.
